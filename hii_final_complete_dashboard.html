Abstract

Healthcare inefficiency represents one of the most critical challenges facing modern healthcare systems, with the United States alone experiencing over $760 billion annually in wasteful spending. This comprehensive analytics platform introduces the Healthcare Inefficiency Index (HII), a novel composite metric designed to quantify and identify resource waste that fails to improve patient outcomes. Through advanced data analytics, predictive modeling, and interactive business intelligence dashboards, this system analyzed 50 hospitals across 5 major procedure types, identifying $125 million+ in potential annual savings opportunities.
